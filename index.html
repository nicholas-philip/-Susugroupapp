How to Open Your Advantage Account?

Anyone with a U.S. checking account can easily begin saving by transferring funds to an Advantage account to achieve their financial goals.

  1. Sign up for your Advantage account online.
  2. Provide your details for verification, and once approved, you'll receive online registration instructions.
  3. After verification, follow the steps to activate your high-yield Advantage savings account. Link your current bank to start saving with your Advantage account.

Gain An Advantage to Your Financial Dreams

Advantage Direct Savings – a financial service of FVCbank, an FDIC-insured institution – is a fee-free savings account with a high annual percentage yield (APY) that helps you grow your savings securely. Banking with Advantage Direct Savings comes with exceptional service and performance:

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a savings plan?

A savings plan is a strategy designed to help you set aside a portion of your income regularly to achieve financial goals, such as purchasing a home or building an emergency fund.

How do I start saving?

To start saving, sign up for a savings account on our platform, set your savings goals, and decide how much you want to contribute each month.

Is my money safe?

Yes, your money is safe. We work with trusted financial institutions to ensure your funds are secure, and all transactions are encrypted for your protection.

Can I withdraw my savings at any time?

Yes, you can withdraw your savings anytime. However, for long-term savings goals, we recommend maintaining your contributions to maximize growth.
